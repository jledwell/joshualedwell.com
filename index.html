professional

I make software and web applications easy, productive, and fun to use. I've employed everything from pencil and paper to HTML / CSS, creating functional prototypes, mockups, personas, use cases, wireframes. In addition, I script, run, and report on usability tests and surveys.

I've worked in consulting and permanent roles for companies including Autodesk, Hewlett-Packard, Lycos.com, Salary.com, and Staples.

I have experience delivering great user experience design on an Agile team. I am also an Agile product owner.
